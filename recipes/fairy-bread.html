Fairy Bread

Description

Fairy bread is sliced white bread spread with butter and covered with "Hundreds and Thousands", often served at children's parties in Australia and New Zealand. It is typically cut into triangles.

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf of white bread
  • butter
  • 100s and 1000s

Steps

  1. Spread a layer of butter over as many slices of white bread as you please.
  2. Sprinkle a generous amount of 100s and 1000s over the bread and butter.
  3. Cut into triangles and serve on a fun plate.
